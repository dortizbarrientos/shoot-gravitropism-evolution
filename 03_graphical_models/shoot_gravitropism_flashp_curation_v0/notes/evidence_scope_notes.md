## Status Language

Use:

- `hypothesis` for unverified or transfer-only ideas with missing direct support.
- `review-supported` only when support comes mainly from review synthesis.
- `primary-paper-supported` only when a primary paper directly supports the edge or a close mechanistic refinement.
- Do not use `curated` yet.
